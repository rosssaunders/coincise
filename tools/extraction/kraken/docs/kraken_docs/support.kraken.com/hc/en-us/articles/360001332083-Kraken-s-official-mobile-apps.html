Where can I use the app?
The app is available in all regions except for Crimea, Donetsk, Luhansk, Cuba, Iran, North Korea and Syria. Some countries are not yet able to use debit or credit cards. Please refer to our FAQ on debit and credit cards for the full list of accepted countries.
Availability in the app store is based on the registered address of your app store account and is not related to the registration of your Kraken account.
How do I download and sign in?
Scan the following QR code to download the Kraken app from Android or iOS app stores:
  • •
    Download Kraken for Android 8.0 and up (Android 9.0 and up is recommended for optimal experience) (Google Play).
  • •
    Download Kraken for iOS 13 and up (Apple App Store).
Setup instructions: You can sign in with your current Kraken account username and password or create and verify a new account in-app. 
Check out our FAQ for the Kraken app here.
