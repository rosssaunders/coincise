Where is Kraken licensed or regulated?
We welcome clients from all over the world, with a few specific exceptions due to international and local regulations. Depending on your verified residency, you may have certain restrictions on your account, including with respect to the crypto assets you can buy, sell and deposit. Refer to our Terms of Service for complete information on our eligibility criteria. Some account features are only available at certain verification levels.
Create a Kraken account
Our funding providers may, at their own discretion, deny funding to certain geographical locations.
Some regions are not yet able to use debit or credit cards for funding. Please refer to our FAQ on debit and credit cards for the full list of accepted regions.
As jurisdictions continue to develop cryptocurrency regulatory frameworks for local operations, we engage with regulators globally in order to serve clients in a compliant manner. We currently hold the following registrations, licenses, authorizations and approvals:

Europe

Belgium
In Belgium, Payward Europe Solutions Limited, trading as “Kraken”, has launched Virtual Asset Service Provider Services (VASP Services) through its Irish Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) registration to provide crypto-asset exchange and custody services.
Clients residing in Belgium are subject to the following restrictions:
  • •
    Cannot deposit or trade POL, TREMP, and XMR.
  • •
    USDG Rewards is not available to clients residing in Belgium.
Cyprus
In Cyprus, Payward Europe Digital Solutions (CY) Limited is regulated by CYSEC as an investment firm with licence number 342/17, authorised to offer certain investment and ancillary services.
  • •

France
In France, Coin Meester B.V., a member of the Kraken group companies, trading as “Kraken”, is registered as a Digital Asset Service Provider (DASP) with the Autorité des Marchés Financiers (AMF) (Registration number E2022-058) to offer digital assets custody, purchase/sale of digital assets for legal tender and exchange of digital assets for other digital assets.
Clients residing in France are subject to the following restrictions:
  • •
    Cannot deposit or trade TREMP, and XMR.
  • •
    USDG Rewards restrictions: USDG Rewards is not available to clients residing in France.
Germany
In Germany, Kraken offers its services through DLT Finance, a brand of DLT Securities GmbH and DLT Custody GmbH trading as “Kraken powered by DLT”. DLT Securities GmbH is regulated as an investment firm by the German Federal Financial Supervisory Authority (BaFin) and DLT Custody GmbH was granted a preliminary Crypto Asset Custodian License (§ 64y Para. 1 KWG) by the BaFin.
Clients residing in Germany are subject to the following restrictions:

Derivatives trading restrictions

    Retail clients residing in Germany have a 2x leverage (4x maintenance) restriction on derivative contracts.

Kraken Pay restrictions

  • •
    Kraken Pay is not available to clients residing in Germany.

Margin trading restrictions

  • •
    Fiat and stablecoin margin lending is not available to clients residing in Germany.

Opt-in rewards restrictions

  • •
    Opt-in Rewards is not available for fiat or stablecoins to clients residing in Germany.

Staking restrictions

  • •
    Staking is not available to clients residing in Germany.
  • •
    USDG Rewards restrictions: USDG Rewards is not available to clients residing in Germany.

Cryptocurrency restrictions

  • •
    A full list of supported and unsupported assets can be found here.

NFT restrictions

  • •
    The Kraken NFT Marketplace is no longer available to clients residing in Germany.

United Kingdom
In the United Kingdom, Kraken operates locally as a Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) Registered Cryptoasset Firm (‘Payward Ltd.’, FRN 928768) under the Money Laundering Regulations.
To offer certain crypto asset-related services to eligible clients, Kraken also maintains through its subsidiary ‘Crypto Facilities Ltd.’, a FCA Authorised Investment Firm (FRN 757895).
In October 2023, regulations were imposed by the FCA which resulted in changes to UK clients’ experience of accessing certain Kraken products. For more information visit: Changes for Clients Residing in the UK.
Clients residing in the UK are subject to the following restrictions:

Opt-in reward restrictions:

    Clients residing in the United Kingdom cannot use Opt-in Rewards.

Kraken Pay restrictions:

  • •
    Clients residing in the UK cannot engage in fiat transfers.

Cryptocurrency restrictions: 

  • •
    Cannot deposit or trade NOS, OMNI, TREMP, and XMR.
  • •
    Cannot buy or sell HNT.

Derivatives trading restrictions

  • •
    Retail clients living in the United Kingdom cannot trade derivatives.
