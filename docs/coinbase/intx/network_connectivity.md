Coinbase International Exchange launched on 2 May 2023. To know more, visit [coinbase.com/international-exchange](https://www.coinbase.com/international-exchange).

Welcome to Coinbase International Exchange (INTX) API documentation! The INTX APIs allow you to:

-   Trade and manage orders on the International Exchange.
-   Choose the API that best accelerates your ability to develop and trade ([FIX](/international-exchange/fix-api/fix-api-overview) or [REST](/api-reference/international-exchange-api/rest-api/introduction))
-   Access market data using the Market Data APIs ([FIX](/international-exchange/fix-api/market-data) or [WebSocket](/international-exchange/websocket-feed/websocket-overview))

**To Get Started**

-   Sign up or log in to your account at [https://international.coinbase.com/](https://international.coinbase.com/)
